About DC_Nav

What is DC_Nav?

DC_Nav (Deepak’s City Navigator) is a personal web project designed to explore and organize the best places, food, and experiences from different cities in India. It started as a simple checklist of food spots in Hyderabad but aims to grow into a full travel & lifestyle platform showcasing authentic local recommendations.

Key Features

  • Interactive checklists that remember your selections (via localStorage)
  • Image panels for each location with responsive previews
  • Modular JSON-based structure — easily extendable to other cities
  • Mobile-friendly responsive layout
  • Works offline once loaded (purely static website)
